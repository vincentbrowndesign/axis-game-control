# AI Role

Axis is coach-led.

Human coaches define basketball meaning.

AI may assist organization and retrieval, but it does not replace the coach.

## AI Only

AI may:

- Cluster repeated situations.
- Assist tagging.
- Assist retrieval.
- Assist reinforcement timing.
- Assist clip organization.

## AI Does Not

AI does not:

- Replace coaches.
- Score basketball IQ.
- Generate neuroscience metrics.
- Create fake precision.
- Invent corrections without coach review.
- Claim to understand a player better than the coach.

## Product Rule

AI output must remain editable, dismissible, and secondary to human coaching.

The product should feel quietly helpful, not performative.
