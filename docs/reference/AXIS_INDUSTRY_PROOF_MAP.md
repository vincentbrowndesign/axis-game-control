Audit date: 2026-06-18

## Research Notes

- Nearly every industry analogy needs external research before being used as product proof.
- The repo itself supports that Axis has borrowed names from coaching, evidence, missions, CV, and review workflows, but it does not prove that any borrowed pattern is the right product direction.
- Highest-confidence analogies are structural rather than visual: observation versus hypothesis, evidence tied to a claim, and review leading to a next move.
